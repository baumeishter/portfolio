WEBWIZ

For the course Data Communication and Networking we were tasked to work out a patch plan, rack layout, purchase plan and network map for a fictional company called WebWiz. The main objective of this exercise was providing a structured cabling solution in the network map.

To meet the requirements of the company, we needed these items in our server rack:

  • 1 router
  • 2 servers
  • 3 patch panels
  • 4 switches

This rack was placed in a seperate room behind a secure locked door.

In the network map, we placed 19 PCs and 3 laptops inside 6 office spaces. 6 laptops were placed in a separate office for later use. Each office has 3 wall mounts to connect Ethernet cables to, an access point for WiFi connectivity and a printer. Each office device has a VoIP telephone connected to it. All cables were running from the server rack to the offices (into the wall mounts) were bound together in an organised fashion to reduce clutter.
