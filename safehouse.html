SAFEHOUSE

Near the end of 2017, when I was about 18 years old, me and two other friends founded what would later be known as SAFEHOUSE. The idea to create this new organisation came from the desire for activities (parties) that we would enjoy but couldn't find in Mechelen at that time. We decided to go in head first and "just organise an event" ourselves. No previous experience whatsoever...

Under the motto of PLUR (Peace, Love, Unity and Respect) we aimed to build a digital platform for creatives in combination with a strong social media presence. We wanted to go big or go home. Belgian and Dutch rap music was very big at that time. Vaporwave as an aesthetic was too. We wanted to combine these two into a micro-festival in the heart of the city. We invited several up-and-coming local talents from in and around Mechelen to perform on the Cultuurplein, on the steps of the Academie and in cooperation with De KUUB. That was MALINES ARRIVE in 2019. That one event started this whole journey.

It was a great success. The ball began rolling and we started planning the next event. Then Corona came on stage and halted everything. No the most fun time of my life but we found ways to keep going by organising a monthly radio show in XL Air studio of the RITCS School of Arts Brussels. Two of our members hosted SAFEHOUSE SATELLITE where we invited speakers and DJs.

In 2022 we got invited to co-host the Botermarkt stage on MAANROCK, twice! During this free, annual city festival we got the chance to present some artists of our platform in front of a sizable crowd. The second year, 2023's edition, we even designed the graphics for the stage.

Some other significant things that happened: one of our members opened 015 MECHELEN, a music cafe near the station. That same member also released their debut EP EGO DEATH. Besides that, we were invited to co-organize DE NACHTWINKEL and CLUB DWAAS. Two very interesting takes on nightlife and clubbing. Something that was severly lacking in Mechelen. Still is but that's my opinion.

My last event that I was a part of was 5 YEARS SAFEHOUSE in 2024. A fitting celebration for our 5th birthday. In 2025 I decided to step down from my role and handed over the reins. This 7-ish year journey was a blast but it was time for something new. On the map below you can find some of our highlighted events in Mechelen.
