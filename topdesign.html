TOPDESIGN

TopDesign is a fictional company that is part of the course Routing and Switching. In this exercise we are tasked with designing the entire network infrastructure in accordance with the demands and needs of the company. This exercise is split into 3 parts and is spread over multiple weeks to complete. This exercise balances technical skills with soft skills like communication and team work.

In the first part of the exercise, we receive all the demands from the company and translate that into a basic network map. Based on the patch plan, we organised all devices into their correct location (room) and connected them to their corresponding switch. None of the devices work properly yet, a full configuration of all networking devices comes in the following parts of the exercise.
