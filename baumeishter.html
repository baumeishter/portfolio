BAUMEISHTER

My love for music comes from my parents and video games. Plain and simple. My dad loves rock and metal music. My mom used to be a singer in Thailand (she even released one LP) and always blasted Thai karaoke songs back when I was young. This clash of heavy and funky is rooted in the styles I prefer: electro and (modern) EBM.

The games that inspired me the most were GTA: Vice City and GTA: San Andreas. Besides the violence (yes I was a kid but let's look past that), these games had some of the best fictional radio stations that were just so good and hilarious to listen to. Some of my most vivid memories as a kid were just driving (or flying) around the city and vibing to the many different radio stations.

Now I am a proud resident at WARRADIO and SAFEHOUSE. I'd define my style as militant beats and chants, electronically charged and appropriately suggestive.
